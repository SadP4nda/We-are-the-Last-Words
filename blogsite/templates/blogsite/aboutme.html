About Me

Hello my name is Lara Pena and I am the main editor and creator of the blog called, "We are the last words." I enjoy writing and reading. In my free time, I like to read books listen to all kinds of music, spend time with my friends, and in the fall and spring, run cross country and track. I am a fan of Broadway and I love the authors Jenny Han, Rainbow Rowell, and J.K. Rowling. I created this blog to enforce my 2018 goal, which is to read 20 books throughout the year. I believe that I will surpass it, especially when I will be posting updates on this blog. This blog however, is not just about books. It is also about things I like such as music, writing, reading, theatre, and much more. I am thankful for all the support, and thank you for reading my work.
